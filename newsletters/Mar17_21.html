The 2021 Annual Scientific Meeting is scheduled for August 29 - September 2, 2021 at the Sheraton Denver Downtown Hotel, Denver, Colorado.

  • Advanced Registration will open on April 1, 2021
  • The meeting brochure and Day-at-a-Glance will be available at a later date
  • For those that are currently holding a registration: “We hope those with paid registrations will be able to join us for the August 2021 in Denver, Colorado. Your meeting registration and all related meeting fees will remain active in our system and will be applied to the 2021 (Denver) meeting. If you decide you cannot attend the 2021 meeting in Denver, Colorado, you should contact Sheryl Kildall at (703) 739-2240 x107 - 9:00 AM - 4:00 PM EDT and request a refund.”

More information on the AsMA 91st Annual Scientific Meeting can be found at on the official website!

Chapter elections

Annual elections will be held at the end of April. We highly encourage everyone who is interested in taking on a leadership role to consider applying for an Executive Board position. The following positions are available:

  • President
  • Vice-President
  • Secretary
  • Treasurer
  • Resident in Aerospace Medicine Representative
  • Chapter Development Officer
  • Public Relations Officer
  • Undergraduate Representative
If you have any questions about the responsibilities of any of the positions, feel free to contact Patrick Pischulti.
